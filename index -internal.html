ENGIX PRO Series ROUND TRIM CONE ADJUSTABLE

PRODUCT DESCRIPTION

The ENGIX Round Cone Trim Adjustable Downlight family is a premium range of architectural luminaires designed for precise, high-quality illumination. With wattages from 6W to 41W and outputs up to 3700 lumens, the series delivers exceptional flexibility across commercial, retail, and residential environments. Engineered with adjustable cone trims for directional lighting, the RD series offers multiple beam angles (9°–50°) and color temperature options (2700K–6000K) to suit a wide range of lighting applications.

Available in white or black finishes, these fixtures feature ON/OFF, 0–10V, and DALI dimming compatibility for seamless integration with modern lighting control systems. Rated IP20 or IP65 depending on the model, the ENGIX RD downlights combine refined aesthetics, superior performance, and installation versatility—making them ideal for both ambient and accent lighting solutions.
